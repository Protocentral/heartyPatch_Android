This screen displays all the parameters from the HeartyPatch related to heart rate variability analysis.

  • Heart-rate variability plot shows the heart rate trend and its variance over time.

  • Heart Rate shows the current instantaneous heart rate

  • Mean RR is the mean value of the R-R interval ovver the last 30 samples.

  • Mean RR is the mean value of the R-R interval ovver the last 30 samples.

  • PNN50 is the mean number of times per hour in which the change between two consecutive intervals exceeds 50 msec

  • SDNN is the standard deviation of the R-R intervals.

  • RMSSD is the root mean square differences between successive R-R intervals.
